Sun, Moon, and Rising

Home Sun Moon Rising
In astrology, a person's sun, moon, and rising signs are three of the most important factors that can influence their personality and life path.

The sun sign represents your core personality, your ego, and your basic identity. It is often seen as the dominant influence in astrology, as it represents the sign that the Sun was passing through at the moment of your birth. Your sun sign is said to influence your basic character traits, such as your temperament, values, and general approach to life.

On the other hand, the moon sign represents your emotional nature, your feelings, and your unconscious self. It is determined by the position of the Moon at the time of your birth. Your moon sign is said to influence your emotional needs, your inner world, and your subconscious desires.

Finally, the rising sign, also known as the ascendant, represents the way others see you and how you present yourself to the world. It is determined by the sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at the moment of your birth. Your rising sign is said to influence your appearance, demeanor, and overall approach to life. All three of these signs work together to create a unique astrological profile for each individual, but some astrologers believe that the sun sign is the most influential of the three. However, it's important to note that astrology is a complex and nuanced field, and many other factors can also play a role in shaping a person's personality and life path.

The Zodiacs

Aries

(March 21 - April 19):

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac and is associated with fire energy. People born under this sign are often seen as adventurous, confident, and independent, but can also be impulsive and hot-headed at times.

Taurus

(April 20 - May 20):

Taurus is associated with earth energy and is known for being grounded, reliable, and persistent. People born under this sign are often focused on their material comfort and security, but can also be stubborn and possessive.

Gemini

(May 21 - June 20):

Gemini is associated with air energy and is known for being communicative, curious, and adaptable. People born under this sign are often social butterflies and love to learn and explore new things, but can also be indecisive and restless.

Cancer

(June 21 - July 22):

Cancer is associated with water energy and is known for being nurturing, emotional, and intuitive. People born under this sign are often deeply connected to their family and home life, but can also be moody and sensitive.

Leo

(July 23 - August 22):

Leo is associated with fire energy and is known for being confident, passionate, and charismatic. People born under this sign often have a natural flair for the dramatic and enjoy being the center of attention, but can also be self-centered and stubborn.

Virgo

(August 23 - September 22):

Virgo is associated with earth energy and is known for being practical, analytical, and detail-oriented. People born under this sign are often dedicated to their work and service to others, but can also be perfectionistic and critical.

Libra

(September 23 - October 22):

Libra is associated with air energy and is known for being diplomatic, charming, and romantic. People born under this sign often seek balance and harmony in their relationships and surroundings, but can also be indecisive and avoidant of conflict.

Scorpio

(October 23 - November 21):

Scorpio is associated with water energy and is known for being intense, passionate, and mysterious. People born under this sign often have a deep understanding of the darker aspects of life and are not afraid to confront their own shadows, but can also be jealous and controlling.

Sagittarius

(November 22 - December 21):

Sagittarius is associated with fire energy and is known for being adventurous, optimistic, and philosophical. People born under this sign often have a love of travel and exploration and are always seeking new experiences, but can also be tactless and restless.

Capricorn

(December 22 - January 19):

Capricorn is associated with discipline, ambition, and hard work. Capricorns are known for their practicality and their ability to achieve success through hard work and dedication.

Aquarius

(January 20 - February 18):

Aquarius is associated with innovation, independence, and humanitarianism. Aquarians are known for their unconventional and forward-thinking approach to life, as well as their desire to create positive change in the world.

Pisces

(February 19 - March 20):

Pisces is associated with empathy, intuition, and spirituality. Pisceans are known for their deep emotional sensitivity and their strong connections to the spiritual realm.
